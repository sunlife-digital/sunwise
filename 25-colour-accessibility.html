Colour accessibility

We’re committed to complying with WCAG AAA accessibility standards because that’s good for our business and our customers.

Accessible roundels

Our roundels have been extensively tested to ensure that they present sufficient contrast between background and text colours to help people who have visual disabilities. Only these colour combinations pass WCAG AAA.

Accessible bubbles

Our bubbles have been extensively tested to ensure that they present sufficient contrast between background and text colours to help people who have visual disabilities. Only these colour combinations pass WCAG AAA.

Inaccessible roundels

These combinations of background and content colours are not accessible at any size and must not be used.
