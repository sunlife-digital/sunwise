Inputs and labels

Our form field combinations are responsive and contain a label and an input.

A wide range of inputs are available including email, number, text and URL. Using the correct input type will activate the related keyboard on mobile devices.

Messages

Use our bubbles to create messages for help and tips, errors and warnings.

Help and tips should always be placed below the input. They should be Midnight with white text.

Errors and warnings should always be placed above the input. They should be Ketchup with white text.

Help and tips
We won’t share your email address

Design notes

Help and tips should always be placed below the input.

HTML

<div class="form-group">
<label class="form-group__label">Email address</label>
<input type="email" placeholder="Must contain an @ symbol">
</div>

<div class="bubble bubble--top bubble--midnight">
We won’t share your email address>
</div>
