Introduction

Living Style Guide

As part of our Digital First strategy, our digital experience is key to delivering effective customer interactions and achieving our business goals as a result. Ensuring that we offer unified customer journeys and that the values and design principles associated with our brand are communicated effectively is core to this.

SunWise is a comprehensive Living Style Guide of purposefully-designed elements used across our digital channels. It provides a toolkit of components, hosted in a responsive framework online, and how to apply them in each context.

SunWise ensures we deliver a consistent experience across all digital channels, and that we align with offline channels too, by demonstrating how each element is styled and displayed. It serves as a tool to familiarise those developing and maintaining the site with the components and can be used to quickly and efficiently develop any new requirements.
