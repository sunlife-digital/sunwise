Design principles

Design principles

Straightforward

Just how simple and frictionless can we make things for our customers? What can we remove? Simplify? What can we make more effortless and intuitive?

Measuring success

Design a clean, consistent and intuitive experience that eliminates ambiguity and ensures accessibility for all.

Confident

We’re bright, positive and we exude confidence — which in turn instils confidence. This confidence ties in well with being straightforward — it enables clean, uncluttered, bold design.

Measuring success

Build credibility and strengthen trust by offering reassuringly clear journeys. Give people control and enable them to act efficiently whilst maintaining a human touch.

Memorable

We’re a challenger brand — so we’re not afraid to do things differently if we think it will be good for our customers. That can lead to surprisingly rewarding, magical moments.

Measuring success

Create a fast, well organised site that uses bold, compelling language displayed with eye-catching images that capture the imagination and enables users to resonate with our message.

Considered

We know our over 50s audience but appreciate everyone’s different. We aim to be as approachable as possible for them. Respectful of and appealing to them. We do things with them, not to them.

Measuring success

Demonstrate understanding of our audience and awareness that time is precious through thoughtful, elegant execution that recognises them, anticipates needs and personalises an effortless experience.
