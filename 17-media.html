Media

Responsive web design has meant that we must re-evaluate how we use images, as it’s no longer possible to serve the same image to the many size screen sizes that people use to experience our products and services.

Responsive images

Our CSS stylesheet resizes images to fill the width of their parent container, no matter what their original size. We recommend that landscape images use the 16:9 widescreen format on most modern displays.

Media organism

Use a media organism to easily create a combination of icon, photograph or graphic illustration and associated content. Uses for this combination include; blog or news entry summaries, product summaries or key facts. Content can include headlines, paragraphs, lists or tables.
