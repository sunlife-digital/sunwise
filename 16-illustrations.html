Graphic illustrations

We use graphic illustrations to add personality to our content, to compliment a message and to lead someone’s eye around a page.

They serve a different purpose to icons, whose purpose is more functional, although they share some visual characteristics. As they contain more detail and depth, graphic illustrations should be reproduced much larger than icons.

Background colours

Graphic illustrations may be reproduced with or without a background circle. Use a tint of Smoke for the background colour. Our recommended tint is 10%.

Smoke background tints

  • 65% #boa7a1
  • 25% #d5d0cd
  • 10% #f2f1f0

Tools and social

We use a light tint of our Smoke supporting colour for the background circles behind graphic illustrations.

Responsive icon sizes

Use the Scalable Vector Graphics (SVG) format so that illustrations may be scaled to any size with no loss of fidelity.

Our illustrations are based on the Kameleon icon set by Webalys. Designers and developers should purchase their own license. We recommend the Designer Pack that contains 1500 icons.
