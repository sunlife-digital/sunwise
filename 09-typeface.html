Typographic scale

We use Modular Scale to create our typographic scale. It’s 5:6 minor third, 1:1.25 Our base font size is 18px/1.8rem (0) and we change sizes by moving up and down the scale: -3 – +7.

  • a 9.219px 0.92rem -3
  • a 11.52px 1.22rem -2
  • a 14.4px 1.44rem -1
  • a 18px 1.8rem 0
  • a 22.5px 2.25rem 1
  • a 28.12px 2.81rem +2
  • a 35.15px 3.51rem +3
  • a 43.94px 4.39rem +4
  • a 54.93px 5.49rem +5
  • a 68.66px 6.86rem +6
  • a 85.83px 8.58rem +7

We use a limited number of type sizes that work together and help us maintain a consistency across different size and types of screen.
