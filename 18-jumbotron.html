Jumbotron

Our Jumbotrons make a great first impression. They introduce people to our business and our products and services.

Anatomy of a Jumbotron

Jumbotrons use a small number of standard elements; A headline, introduction text and a call-to-action. Jumbotron content should be clear and consise as it may be the only thing a person reads on a page.

Background colour

Use Sky Jumbotrons for key product pages, for example Guaranteed Over 50 Plan.

Choosing a Jumbotron image

Choose Jumbotron images that people can either identify with, exemplify and amplify the meaning of the content you’re presenting or preferably both.

Jumbotron image position

Place images to the right of a Jumbotron in order to preserve a clean left reading edge. Allow images to bleed out of a Jumbotron to draw the eye inside towards the content.

Responsive Jumbotron

Jumbotrons respond to the different orientations and sizes of screens that people use to use to learn about our products and services.

Large screen Jumbotron

On large screens, our content occupies 6 columns of our 12 column grid but with a 1 column offset. Column 1 is left empty to visually balance the image on the right. This background image is aligned 100% right to help avoid it overlapping the content.

Slimline large screen Jumbotron

Occassionally we may use alternative, slimline Jumbotron, for example on pages which contain important product information but which, for whatever reason, don't offer an onwards purchase journey – such as Wills.

Medium screen Jumbotron

On medium screens, our content occupies 7 columns of our grid with no offset. A closer crop of the background image swapped in and it is reduced in size to aid faster page loads on mobile devices.

Small screen Jumbotron

On small screens, our content is presented in a single column (occupies the full 12 columns of the Bootstrap grid). For reasons of performance we don’t include an image at this size.

Whitespace Jumbotron

Not every page needs a full Jumbotron, sometimes less is more. Adding generous amounts of whitespace introduces a pause, slows down someone’s reading and helps them to focus. It also helps to give our presentation a quality feel.

We use our Sky Jumbotrons for pages where there's a transcational or product emphasis and whitespace Jumbotrons for all other, more informational, pages.

Support page Jumbotron

Help our support pages to express our personality by using one of our decorative Jumbotron images.
