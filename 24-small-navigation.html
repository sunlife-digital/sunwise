Small navigation

Small navigation helps people to understand where they are within our website and where to go next.

Breadcrumb

  1. Home
  2. Life cover
  3. Guaranteed Over 50 Plan

Design notes

Don’t use on pages directly accessible from our main navigation. There’s also no need to include the current page.

HTML

<ol class="breadcrumb">
<li class="breadcrumb-item"><a href="">Home</a></li>
<li class="breadcrumb-item"><a href="">Life cover</a></li>
<li class="breadcrumb-item active">Guaranteed Over 50 Plan</li>
</ol>
