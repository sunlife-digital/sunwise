Roundels

Use Ketchup for the background of roundels when you need to strongly emphasise information and for price information on Sunshine roundels.

Icons with consequences

Pressing an icon can have consequences such as deleting an item or closing a window. Use Ketchup to warn people before they press.

Buttons

Pressing a button can have consequences such as cancelling a selection. Use Ketchup to warn people before they press.

Form elements

Helpful feedback is essential to a good user experience. Use Ketchup to indicate an error or any form label or input.
