Bubbles

The ability to highlight a key benefit or message is important. We want everyone to know why they’re buying from us and how we can help them get on with life. Our bubbles are great at emphasising the key messages in our content.

Bubble tails

Bubbles are rounded rectangles. They have a tail which we use to point to the content or functionality we’re emphasising. Place a tail either above or below a bubble.

Bubble base colours

Bubbles are ‘sunshine elements’ and we use them to connect important information to our brand. We use our Sunshine colour for the background of standard bubbles. The content they contain is Midnight. Use Ketchup text to present price information.

Sky panels

To create sufficient colour contrast use only Sunshine or Midnight bubbles. White replaces ketchup in this context.

Bubble text colours

In Sunshine bubbles, use Ketchup to present price and offer information. For example “Free” or “£4.” Otherwise we use Midnight.

Accessible bubbles

Our bubbles have been extensively tested to ensure that they present sufficient contrast between background and text colours to help people who have visual disabilities. Only these colour combinations pass WCAG AAA.
